Kept is a private, local-first expense tracker. Every number you enter stays on your device — there is no account to create, no server to sync to, and nothing to track. It is the simple, honest way to see where your money goes.

WHY KEPT

• Truly private by design. There is no backend. Your data lives in an on-device database and never leaves your phone. No sign-up, no email, no cloud.
• No ads, ever. No trackers, no analytics, no selling your data — because none of it is collected in the first place.
• Fast and offline. Works completely offline. Open the app, log an expense, done.

FEATURES

• Log both income and expenses with a clean, quick entry screen.
• Multi-currency support with correct, currency-aware rounding.
• Organise spending with categories and tags.
• A running ledger of everything, grouped by date.
• At-a-glance stats to see where your money goes.
• CSV import and export — your data is yours to take anywhere.
• Full backup and restore.
• App lock with a PIN, plus optional biometric (fingerprint / face) unlock.
• Light and dark themes.

YOUR DATA, YOUR DEVICE

Kept is built on a simple principle: your spending is nobody's business but yours. There is no cloud account, because there is no cloud. If you want your data elsewhere, export a CSV. If you want it gone, wipe it in-app or just uninstall — there is no remote copy anywhere, because there never was one.

Free, private, and refreshingly simple.

## Data safety form

Play's definition of "collect": user data transmitted **off the device**. On-device-only
processing that is never sent off the device is **not** collection. This app sends nothing
off the device, so the answers are almost entirely "No".

### Data collection and security
- **Does your app collect or share any of the required user data types?**
  → **No**

  (Selecting No skips the entire data-type matrix. Nothing is transmitted off the device:
  expenses, categories, tags, settings, and the salted PIN hash all stay in on-device storage.)

Because you answered No, these follow-ups are not shown / are not applicable:
- Encryption in transit — N/A (no data leaves the device).

### Data deletion
- **Do you provide a way for users to request that their data is deleted?**
  → Not applicable — no data is collected or stored off-device. Users can erase all local
  data at any time via the in-app "wipe and start over" option, or by uninstalling the app.

  (If the form requires a response anyway, choose the option indicating data is not collected /
  users can delete data on-device; there is no account and no server-side copy to request deletion of.)
