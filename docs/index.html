Everything you need

A complete expense tracker, minus the cloud

Built for the 5-second entry you make many times a day — then powerful enough to slice a year of spending by category, tag and date.

Truly local-first

Every entry is stored in on-device SQLite. There's no backend to breach and no account to leak — the app works fully offline, forever.

PIN & biometric lock

A PIN gates the whole app, with optional fingerprint or Face unlock and a short grace period so quick app-switches don't nag you.

5-second entry

One tap on the FAB, a Debit/Credit toggle that recolors live, a numeric keypad and smart category defaults. Duplicate a recurring expense in two taps.

Categories & tags

Thirteen preloaded categories plus your own, and free-form tags on every entry. Colour-coded chips keep the ledger scannable at a glance.

Filter & search

Combine a category, multiple tags (restrictive AND) and free-text search to answer questions like "how much coffee at work this month?"

Stats & charts

Monthly totals, a by-category breakdown, spend-over-time bars and a custom total-by-tags query — so the numbers actually tell you something.

You own the backup

Export a full-fidelity JSON backup or a CSV of entries, and import legacy history back in. Your data is portable because it's yours.

One codebase, every screen

Built with Expo & React Native — the same app runs on Android, iOS and the web, with a clean light and dark theme on all three.

Undo & soft delete

Delete gives you an instant Undo, entries stay recoverable for 30 days, and dates never drift — an expense on the 4th is the 4th, in any timezone.

Privacy by architecture

Your data never leaves your device

This isn't a privacy policy promise — it's how the app is built. There is simply no server for your spending to be sent to. Nothing to opt out of, because nothing is collected.

  • No backend, no account. You never sign up or log in. The lock is a local PIN, not a cloud identity.
  • Zero telemetry. No analytics, crash-reporting or tracking SDKs that phone home. The app makes no network calls with your data.
  • Portable & yours. Export a JSON backup any time and restore it into a fresh install with identical data.
  • Open source. Don't take our word for it — read exactly what the app does, line by line, on GitHub.
